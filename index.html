Hydraulics becomes the NEW Danfoss Power Solutions

With the recent Hydraulics sale to Danfoss, we are forming the NEW Danfoss Power Solutions to become an even stronger global leader in mobile and industrial hydraulics, serving our customers and partners even better than before.

We are Stronger Together.
We are the NEW Danfoss Power Solutions.

This is a new, exciting chapter for both our businesses - the bridging of two very strong hydraulics manufacturers.

Together we are creating a global leader in mobile and industrial hydraulics.

We both have common roots – a long, proud history in hydraulics. Our product and market coverage are complementary, with the most comprehensive product portfolio across mobile and industrial hydraulics and broadened local customer reach to better serve our customers and distributors. Together, we will continue to have superior channel strength, an experienced team of engineers to partner with and a localized sales force to serve our customer even better than before.

The focus for the first 100 days is maintaining businses continuity and stability. We want to ensure this transition is as smooth and seamless as possible for our customers, partners and our people.
